What We Do

Complete Elevator Solutions

From design to installation, maintenance to modernization — we handle it all.

Passenger Elevators

Smooth, silent, and safe passenger lifts for residential apartments, offices, malls, and high-rise buildings — engineered for comfort and reliability.

  • Up to 30 floors capacity
  • Energy-saving VFD drives
  • Custom cabin designs

Freight & Cargo Elevators

Heavy-duty freight lifts designed for warehouses, factories, and logistics centres — capable of handling massive loads with precision control.

  • Capacity up to 10,000 kg
  • Robust steel construction
  • Industrial-grade controls

Hospital / MRL Elevators

Machine-room-less (MRL) and hospital-grade lifts for healthcare facilities — hygienic, whisper-quiet, and fast for critical environments.

  • Stretcher-ready dimensions
  • Emergency power backup
  • Anti-microbial surfaces

Home / Villa Lifts

Compact and elegant home elevators that blend seamlessly into your interior — perfect for villas, bungalows, and duplex homes.

  • Panoramic glass options
  • Space-saving design
  • Low pit depth required

Escalators & Moving Walks

High-traffic escalators and travelators for shopping centres, airports, and metro stations — engineered for durability and passenger flow.

  • 30°/35° incline options
  • VVVF speed control
  • Safety comb-plate sensors

Dumbwaiters

Compact service lifts for restaurants, hotels, and libraries — efficiently transporting food, documents, and small goods between floors.

  • 50–300 kg capacity
  • Stainless steel interior
  • Silent operation

Maintenance & AMC

Annual Maintenance Contracts (AMC) with scheduled inspections, preventive servicing, and priority emergency breakdown response 24/7.

  • Monthly inspections
  • 2-hour response time
  • Genuine spare parts

Modernization & Upgrades

Breathe new life into old elevators — upgrade controls, cabins, drives, and safety systems to meet modern standards without full replacement.

  • Control system upgrades
  • Cabin refurbishment
  • Safety code compliance

Safety Inspection & Audit

Independent safety inspections and certification audits — ensuring your lifts meet all regulatory requirements and international safety standards.

  • Load testing
  • Compliance certification
  • Full audit reports
